# Bhavya Investments AI CRM

> Enterprise-grade AI CRM for Financial Advisors, Insurance Consultants, Mutual Fund Distributors, and Real Estate Professionals.

---

# Vision

Bhavya Investments AI CRM is designed to become a complete business operating system for financial advisors.

The platform will unify:

- Lead Management
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Mutual Fund Operations
- Insurance Management
- Property Management
- AI Assistant
- Voice Calling
- WhatsApp Automation
- Email Automation
- Calendar & Task Management
- Analytics & Reporting

The objective is to reduce manual work, improve customer experience, and automate business operations.
